Privacy Policy

We collect nothing.

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

No data collected. nutrx does not collect, transmit, or store any data outside your device.
No accounts. You never create an account or provide any personal information to us.
No analytics. We have no telemetry, crash reporting, or usage tracking of any kind.
No ads. nutrx is not ad-supported and never will be.
No third-party SDKs. There are no third-party libraries or services embedded in the app.

Overview

nutrx is a privacy-first iOS application for tracking daily nutrient intake. This policy explains how we handle your information — which, to be direct, we do not handle at all.

nutrx is built by nutrx Labs. We believe your health data is yours and yours alone. The app is designed from the ground up so that no data ever leaves your device, full stop.

Data we collect

None. nutrx does not collect any personal data, health data, usage data, or any other information from you or your device.

Everything you enter into nutrx — your nutrient entries, daily logs, personal profile, and history — is stored exclusively in the app's local SwiftData database on your iPhone or iPad. This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

Internet access

nutrx does not require an internet connection and makes no network requests of any kind during normal use. The app functions entirely offline. There is no backend server, no API, and no cloud infrastructure associated with nutrx.

Analytics and crash reporting

nutrx contains no analytics, telemetry, event tracking, or crash reporting tools. We have no visibility into how you use the app, how often you open it, or whether it crashes. If you encounter a bug, please reach out to us directly via the support page.

Third-party services

nutrx contains no third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, or external services. The only software running in the app is code we wrote ourselves, built on Apple's native iOS frameworks.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it at all times. To delete all nutrx data, simply delete the app from your device. There is no account to close, no server-side data to request deletion of, and no one to contact — because we never had your data to begin with.

Children's privacy

nutrx does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted, nutrx is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations by design.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how nutrx handles data — for example, if a future Pro feature introduces optional cloud sync — we will update this policy and clearly communicate the change before it takes effect. The current version date is always shown at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how nutrx works, you can reach us at support@nutrx-labs.com. We're a small team and we read every message.
