Why nutrx

Built for real life.
Not for engagement.

Private by design

All your data lives exclusively on your device. No accounts, no servers, no cloud sync. Your health data is yours — period.

Fully customisable

Define your own nutrients, units, step sizes, and daily targets. Track anything you want — the way you actually want to track it.

Zero friction logging

Tap + to log. Swipe to customise. Long press for extras. Logging should take seconds — and with nutrx, it does.

Works fully offline

No internet connection required — ever. nutrx works anywhere, any time, with zero dependency on external services or servers.

Full history

Browse every past day at a glance. Every intake entry is stored locally — nothing is ever deleted automatically.

Free forever

All core tracking features are free and will stay that way. A Pro tier is coming for AI-powered extras — but the fundamentals stay open.
