Common questions

Is my data backed up anywhere? ▾

No — and that's by design. nutrx stores everything exclusively on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server. If you delete the app, your data is gone, so treat it like any other local file.

Can I track any nutrient I want? ▾

Yes. nutrx has no preset list. You define your own nutrients, units, step sizes, and daily targets from scratch. Vitamin D in IU, caffeine in mg, water in glasses — whatever makes sense for you.

Does nutrx work without an internet connection? ▾

Completely. nutrx is fully offline — there's no network dependency at all. It works the same on a plane as it does at home.

Is nutrx free? ▾

All core tracking features are free and will stay that way, forever. A Pro tier is planned for AI-powered extras like insights and personalised recommendations — but logging, history, and everything you use daily will never be gated.

What iOS version do I need? ▾

nutrx targets the latest stable version of iOS. We recommend keeping your device up to date for the best experience.
